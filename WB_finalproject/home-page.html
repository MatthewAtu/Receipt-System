Our Story

Our story begins with "Willy James" back in 1998. He quit his job at "Untitled Goose Inc."

to pursue his passion for professional burger making. Starting with a small food cart in his hometown,

Willy focused on using fresh ingredients and simple recipes. Word spread quickly, and soon he opened his first restaurant.

Today, his burger chain is known across the province for its commitment to quality and great taste. John's dedication to his craft continues to inspire everything we do.
